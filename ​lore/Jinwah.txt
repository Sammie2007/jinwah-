Jinwah: The Voidheart
​Born of a forbidden union between a High Priestess of the Light Disciples and a rogue Void-Walker, Jinwah exists as a living contradiction. They possess the "Voidheart"—a rare metaphysical core that can absorb both blinding light and absolute darkness.
​While the factions see Jinwah as a weapon to be claimed or a mistake to be erased, Jinwah seeks "The Third Path," a legendary state of balance that could stop the cosmic war. Jinwah’s physical appearance shifts depending on their emotional state: their eyes glow with a cyan void-light when calm, but spark with volatile golden embers when pushed to the brink of survival.
